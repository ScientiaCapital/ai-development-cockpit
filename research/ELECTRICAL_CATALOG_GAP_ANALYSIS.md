# Electrical Catalog Gap Analysis - Multi-Segment MEP Contractor

**Research Date**: 2026-01-13
**Current Catalog Items**: ~210 total (~34 Electrical items)
**Purpose**: Identify GAPS for full-service electrical contractors across all market segments

---

## Executive Summary

The existing catalog has a strong foundation for **residential electrical** but significant gaps in:
1. **Commercial & Industrial (C&I)** - Missing standard electrical infrastructure
2. **Industrial/Utility** - Missing medium voltage, motor control, studies
3. **Wire/Conduit/Materials** - Almost no commodity materials
4. **Specialized Services** - Missing code studies, thermal imaging, testing

**Estimated Items to Add**: 180-220 new electrical catalog items
