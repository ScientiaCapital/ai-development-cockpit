## RESIDENTIAL PROJECTS

### HVAC - Residential

#### Permits & Approvals
- **Building Permit** - Required for HVAC equipment replacement/installation
- **Mechanical Permit** - City/county mechanical permit for HVAC work
- **HOA Approval** - Homeowners Association approval for exterior equipment
- **Utility Interconnect Agreement** - For grid-tied systems (heat pumps with battery)

#### Insurance & Bonding
- **General Liability Certificate** - $1M/$2M standard for residential
- **Workers Compensation Certificate** - Required if crew size > 1

#### Compliance & Certifications
- **EPA 608 Certification** - Refrigerant handling (technician certification)
- **NATE Certification** - North American Technician Excellence (optional but preferred)

#### Contracts & Agreements
- **Residential HVAC Contract** - Scope, price, payment terms, warranty
- **Service Agreement** - Ongoing maintenance contract (PM visits)

#### Financial Documents
- **Quote/Proposal** - Itemized pricing for equipment + labor
- **Invoice** - Final billing after completion
- **Warranty Registration** - Manufacturer warranty (equipment + labor)

#### Technical Documents
- **Load Calculation** - Manual J (ACCA standard for proper sizing)
- **Equipment Specifications** - Model numbers, SEER ratings, tonnage
- **Ductwork Design** - Manual D (if duct modifications required)

#### Inspection & Testing
- **Final Inspection Report** - City/county sign-off on permit
- **Refrigerant Charge Verification** - Superheat/subcooling measurements
- **Airflow Measurement** - CFM at registers, static pressure

#### Operations & Maintenance
- **Homeowner Manual** - Equipment operation, filter replacement
- **Service Plan Enrollment** - Preventive maintenance schedule
- **Warranty Documentation** - Manufacturer + contractor warranties

- **Quote/Proposal** - System size (kW), annual production (kWh), cost

- **Structural Engineering Letter** - Roof can support solar load

- **Homeowner Manual** - Monitoring app, system operation
- **Warranty Documentation** - 25-year panel, 10-year inverter, workmanship
- **Monitoring System Access** - Enphase, SolarEdge, Tesla app credentials
