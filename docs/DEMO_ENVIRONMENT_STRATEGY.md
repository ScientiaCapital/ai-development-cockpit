## The "No Admin" Rule Explained

### Why We NEVER Give Prospects Admin Role

**Admin can**:
- Delete all data (destroy the demo environment)
- Change company settings (break configurations)
- Delete other users (remove our demo employees)
- Access payment integrations (see Stripe/QuickBooks credentials)
- Change workflows (mess up our carefully configured processes)
- Archive forms, templates, automations
- Access API keys, webhooks, integrations

**One bad prospect** with Admin access = demo environment destroyed = hours of rebuild work

---

### What If Prospect Wants to See Company Settings?

**Option 1: Screen Share (You Drive)**
- "Let me show you company settings on screen share"

**Option 2: Screenshots/Video**
