## How to Enter a New Vertical

### Option A: Get a Job There (Best Learning)
1. Join a SaaS serving that vertical
2. Learn for 6-12 months on their dime
3. Build templates as part of your job
4. Productize on the side

### Option B: Consulting Gig
1. Land a consulting project in the vertical
2. Deep dive for 2-3 months
3. Build templates as deliverables
4. Keep rights to generalized versions

### Option C: Research + Build
1. Interview 10-20 practitioners in the vertical
2. Study competitor SaaS platforms
3. Build templates based on research
4. Sell to SaaS companies (harder without proof)

## What Makes a Good Vertical?

### Must-Haves
- [ ] **Lots of forms/templates** - Paper-heavy industries
- [ ] **Regulated** - Compliance = templates have value
- [ ] **Fragmented SaaS market** - Many potential buyers
- [ ] **Recurring work** - Service plans, maintenance

### Nice-to-Haves
- [ ] **You're interested in it** - Passion = better product
- [ ] **Growing industry** - More SaaS entrants = more buyers
- [ ] **High switching costs** - Templates lock in customers
- [ ] **Poor existing solutions** - Room to differentiate
