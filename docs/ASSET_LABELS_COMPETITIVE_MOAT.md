## The Procore Comparison

### What Procore Does Well
- Construction project management
- Subcontractor coordination
- Document control
- RFIs and submittals
- Large commercial project oversight

### What Procore CAN'T Do
- **Track equipment after project closeout** - Once the project is done, Procore forgets the equipment exists
- **Manage warranties** - No concept of warranty periods on installed equipment
- **Schedule preventive maintenance** - No PM scheduling based on equipment age
- **Monitor system performance** - No integration with solar inverters, HVAC controls, etc.
- **Generate service revenue** - Procore ends when the project ends

### Demo Killer Line for Procore Users
> "Procore is great for the 6-month installation project. Coperniq is great for the 20-year relationship with that customer's equipment. Which generates more lifetime revenue?"
